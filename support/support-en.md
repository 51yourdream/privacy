## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What should I do if the application crashes?
A: Please try the following solutions:
1. Restart the application
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest version
4. Check device storage space

### Q: How to restore purchases?
A: Click the "Restore Purchases" button in the app, make sure you're logged in with the same Apple ID.

### Q: What if the app cannot load content?
A: Please check your network connection, try switching networks (WiFi/mobile data), and restart the app.
